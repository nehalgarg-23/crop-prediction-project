Crop Information

Rice

Rice is a staple food for a large portion of the world's population, requiring a hot and humid climate.

Maize

Maize, or corn, is a versatile crop used as food, feed, and raw material in various industries.

Jute

Jute is used in making ropes, sacks, and mats, thriving in regions with high rainfall.

Cotton

Cotton is a vital crop for the textile industry, requiring a warm climate for growth.

Coconut

Coconut is used for food, oil, and coir, thriving in tropical areas with plenty of rainfall.

Papaya

Papaya is a tropical fruit rich in vitamins and antioxidants, requiring warm climates.

Orange

Oranges are juicy citrus fruits high in vitamin C, thriving in subtropical climates.

Apple

Apples are popular fruits requiring a temperate climate for cultivation.

Muskmelon

Muskmelons are refreshing fruits, rich in vitamins A and C, grown during warm seasons.

Watermelon

Watermelons are sweet, juicy fruits perfect for hot climates, rich in water and nutrients.

Grapes

Grapes are grown for wine, juice, and consumption, requiring a warm climate with moderate humidity.

Mango

Mango, the "King of Fruits," is a tropical delight needing plenty of sunlight and warmth.

Banana

Bananas are a staple fruit, grown in warm and humid regions, rich in potassium.

Pomegranate

Pomegranates are valued for their seeds and juice, requiring dry and subtropical climates.

Lentil

Lentils are high-protein legumes grown in temperate climates with moderate water needs.

Blackgram

Blackgram is a legume widely used in Indian cuisine, thriving in warm regions.

Mungbean

Mungbeans are nutritious legumes, rich in protein, grown in warm climates.

Mothbeans

Mothbeans are drought-resistant legumes grown in arid and semi-arid regions.

Pigeonpeas

Pigeonpeas are widely grown for their protein-rich seeds, requiring warm climates.

Kidneybeans

Kidneybeans are a staple legume, grown in temperate climates, rich in protein and fiber.

Chickpea

Chickpeas are versatile legumes, grown in dry climates, popular for making hummus.

Coffee

Coffee is a globally loved beverage crop, grown in tropical regions with specific altitudes.
